Section 10

What happens next

Once your form is received we create a secure case folder, save a PDF copy of this application, and send a secure upload link to the primary contact and broker only.

  1. Complete and submit this application form.
  2. We create a secure folder for your application.
  3. A PDF copy of this form is saved into your case folder.
  4. We email the primary contact and broker a secure upload link for ID, income, bank and loan statements.
  5. We review your situation and discuss suitable lender options with you.

Automated emails will come from your applications mailbox once the live process is connected.

Privacy consent

Collection, use and disclosure of your information

Upswing collects the personal, financial and credit-related information in this application so we can assess your enquiry, prepare your application, communicate with you, and submit or discuss it with relevant lenders, aggregators, service providers, credit reporting bodies, valuers, insurers, solicitors and accountants involved in arranging or assessing finance.
  • Information may include identity, contact, income, expenses, assets, liabilities, employment, entity, property and security details, and supporting documents.
  • We use it to verify details, assess options, meet legal and compliance obligations, manage your file and contact you about this application.
  • We may disclose relevant information to lenders and service providers so they can assess and process the application.
  • You can ask to access or correct your information, or ask how it’s handled, by contacting Upswing.
